About AMG

AMG Constructions offer over 30 years of experience in delivering quality home construction and superior end results to Melbourne’s southern and eastern suburbs. We maintain a close relationship with our clients and work within set budgets and timeframes from concept to creation. This is all without ever compromising on innovative designs and outstanding quality whether you are looking for a brand new build, a renovation or an extension. Our difference is the care we take to guarantee that our clients are informed every step of the build journey to see that all needs and expectations are met. We are proud to say that a majority of our work originates from word-of-mouth and the glowing recommendations from happy clients. If you are after personal, friendly service with proven results, please see below for more information or come and see us in our office today!
