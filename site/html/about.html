ABOUT US

AMG Constructions prides itself in not only building quality constructions (new build/extensions/renovations) but building quality relationships with the clientele. This ensures devotion to all aspects of the build, from initial quoting to drafting, to complete construction to handover; keeping the clientele’s needs and expectations at the forefront. The company’s ultimate philosophy is to deliver quality work, within budget and within timeframes. Work will always be completed at the highest quality, and that’s a guarantee.

Andrew Greenall (Andy) is a Registered Builder Practitioner (No. CDB-U 52073) and Masters Builders Association Member (028469). He has been in the building/construction business for over 30 years, and is a qualified carpenter by trade.

Andy is the Director and owner of AMG Constructions Pty. Ltd. with its head office located in Mount Dandenong.
